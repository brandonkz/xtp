## 1. Core Positioning

Yeet should not try to win by looking like a louder version of Stake.

The best wedge is:

- **Safest place to gamble in crypto**
- **Built for serious players, not just bonus tourists**
- **Listed group / audited / segregated-funds trust angle**
- **A place whales feel comfortable leaving real balances**

This means the growth system should be built around:

1. **Trust conversion**
2. **VIP migration**
3. **Creator distribution**
4. **High-intent content and social proof**
5. **Retention mechanics for valuable players**

---

## 2. Ideal Customer Profiles

### A. VIP Whale
- Large weekly or monthly wager volume
- Already active on Stake / Rollbit / Roobet / Shuffle
- Cares about host quality, withdrawal handling, comp treatment, and balance safety
- Often complains less about games and more about treatment

### B. Serious Mid-High Roller
- Wants strong rewards and fast withdrawals
- Active on Crypto Twitter, Telegram, Discord, Reddit
- Responds to comparison pages, streamer trust, and proof-of-payouts

### C. Creator-Led Recreational Player
- Comes through streamers, clips, creator pages, or community hype
- Needs a clear reason to sign up now
- Converts better on creator-specific landing pages than on generic homepage traffic

---

## 3. Operating Principles

### Principle 1: Sell trust before bonuses
The real question in crypto gambling is not "what's the bonus?"
It is: **"Will I get paid and treated properly?"**

### Principle 2: Steal, don't wait
A lot of growth in this category is simply capturing dissatisfied users from competitors fast.

### Principle 3: Build migration funnels, not just acquisition funnels
For high-value players, the enemy is restart friction:
- losing VIP status
- not knowing if support is serious
- uncertainty around limits and withdrawals

### Principle 4: Use specificity as a weapon
Generic casino marketing is weak.
Specific messaging wins:
- faster withdrawal handling
- tier matching
- better host access
- safer treasury story
- cleaner comp structure

### Principle 5: Treat retention as growth
Reloads, tier nudges, winback, missions, and host-led outreach are often more profitable than cold acquisition.

## 4. Top Priority Growth Plays

## 4.1 VIP Tier-Match Funnel

### Offer
- Tier match from Stake / Rollbit / Roobet / Shuffle
- Dedicated VIP manager
- Priority withdrawals
- Custom lossback / cashback structure
- White-glove onboarding

### Assets needed
- Tier-match landing page
- Submission form
- Telegram / Signal concierge handoff
- Comparison table vs major competitors
- Internal qualification rules

### Why it matters
This is the cleanest way to remove switching friction for serious players.

### KPI
- VIP lead count
- VIP qualified lead rate
- VIP first deposit rate
- 30-day VIP retention
- average VIP deposit and wager volume

---

## 4.2 Competitor Complaint Harvesting

### Goal
Turn public competitor frustration into Yeet pipeline.

### Where to look
- Replies under Stake, Roobet, Rollbit, Shuffle tweets
- Reddit complaint threads
- Telegram / Discord communities
- Creator chats and clip comments

### Signals to capture
- withdrawal complaints
- VIP / host complaints
- KYC complaints
- geo restriction complaints
- users posting usernames publicly
- users flexing large bet slips or balance screenshots

### Workflow
1. Monitor competitors daily
2. Save handles with reason for outreach
3. Tag by value signal
4. Send tailored outreach, not generic spam
5. Route warm prospects to concierge / VIP host

### Example outreach
"Saw your reply under Stake. If the issue is slow VIP treatment or weak comp response, Yeet is pushing a safer high-touch VIP setup. If useful, I can show you what a tier match would look like."

### Watchout
Do not blast low-signal users. Focus on pain + value signal.

## 4.4 Creator-Specific Landing Pages

### For each creator, build:
- custom page
- custom offer
- creator clips / proof
- preferred games / betting angle
- custom CTA and tracking

### Why it works
Traffic from creators usually performs badly on generic homepages and much better on pages that feel native to the creator.

### Best creator segments
- mid-tier crypto-native streamers
- gambling clippers
- sports-betting crossover creators
- creators with trust-heavy communities

## 4.5 Lossback Calculator + Safe Policy

### Why this matters
Lossback is a strong sales tool, but dangerous if run loosely.

### Policy should include
- weekly caps
- eligible games only
- weighting by house edge
- tier thresholds
- explicit exclusions for low-edge abuse

### Use cases
- VIP sales
- landing pages
- retention nudges
- creator explanations
- outbound DMs

## 6. Retention System

## 6.1 Reloads by timing
Trigger around:
- usual deposit times
- major sporting events
- losing sessions
- inactivity windows
- near-tier thresholds

## 6.2 Mission system
Build missions around:
- play streaks
- cross-sell between sportsbook and casino
- leaderboard participation
- high-margin product usage
- weekly unlocks

## 6.3 VIP near-miss nudges
If someone is close to a new tier, show:
- exact progress
- exact reward difference
- limited-time push to qualify

## 6.4 Smart winback
Segment by reason for inactivity:
- lost too much
- weak host relationship
- better competitor offer
- sportsbook-only dropoff
- casino-only boredom

Then customize the comeback offer.

## 10. Biggest Risks

### Risk 1: Overpromising trust
If the operational experience does not match the trust story, this strategy backfires hard.

### Risk 2: Bonus abuse
Loose lossback and VIP offers can get farmed.

### Risk 3: Spray-and-pray outreach
Bad outbound will damage brand perception quickly.

### Risk 4: Generic creator traffic
Big creator spend without custom landing and offer structure usually leaks money.

### Risk 5: Confusing offer stack
If the site message is messy, users default back to the big incumbents.
